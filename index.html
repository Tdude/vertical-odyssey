Steffo's Vertical Odyssey

You are Mr Steffo, the Red Beard Legend with a slightly Bad Ass. Ascend this treacherous Big Wall. Your legend grows with every perilous meter conquered. Only the tenacious will etch their name into the annals of the Odyssey.

Commands of The Holy Climb:

  • Click Mouse: Reach for and grab a visible handhold. Choose wisely; your reach is mighty, but not infinite!
  • 'P' Key: When clinging to a sturdy crack (the rectangular holds), press 'P' to place a "nut" – so don't leave your cohones at home!

The Perils & Principles:

  • Veiled Holds: Grips shimmer into existence only as you draw near. Anticipate and explore!
  • Fleeting Stability: Every hold is ephemeral! Cling too long, and you will slip with your sweaty little korvfingers. Agility is key.
  • The Fall & Return: A misstep or failed grip sends you plummeting to your last secure protection, or worse. You'll earn no glory until your previous altitude is reclaimed.
  • Vital Essence (Stamina): Each move saps your strength. Exhaustion means an inevitable fall.
  • Precious micro-nuts: Your supply is finite and you're out of cams. Use your micro cohones wisely – the higher you climb, the greater the risk... and the reward.
